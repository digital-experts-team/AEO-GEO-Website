/ 01 Company Overview

Supplier of Chinese Vehicle Brands

Shanghai Global Auto Spare Parts Co. LLC is a Dubai-based supplier and distributor of OEM and aftermarket spare parts for Chinese vehicle brands, serving Dubai, Sharjah, and Abu Dhabi. It sources directly from Chinese manufacturers, covering brands including Changan, Chery, GAC, Geely, JAC, Jetour, Chana, Haval, MG, and GWM.

It operates a main hub plus a newer Abu Dhabi branch, with listed locations in Deira/Abu Hail (Dubai) and an industrial-area branch in Sharjah.

/ 07 Opportunities / Recommendations

Action Plan

  1. Close the desktop-vs-mobile performance gap — mobile PageSpeed data should be pulled fresh and addressed if it lags desktop (desktop currently scores 96/96/100/100).
  2. Build longer-form, question-answering content on existing brand/city pages, matching the depth pattern used by Noorhan Trading.
  3. Pursue a trade-show or industry-event presence/listing — Samdo's Automechanika Dubai 2025 exhibitor status is a credible, linkable authority signal Shanghai Global currently lacks an equivalent to.
  4. Actively generate reviews on all location listings, starting with the Sharjah Yandex listing, which currently shows zero reviews.
