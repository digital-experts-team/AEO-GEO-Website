CASE STUDY Zoople & Webcastle Growth & paid

Full-funnel growth across a client portfolio.

Led both organic traffic growth and paid campaign retargeting across Webcastle's client portfolio and Zoople's admissions funnel — owning SEO intent keyword tracking, on-page optimization, content structuring, and link building, all driving inbound inquiries.

SCOPE

ACCOUNTS

/ The challenge

Multiple brands, one funnel goal: more qualified inbound.

Across an agency client portfolio and an admissions funnel, growth had to come from both sides of the house — organic search that compounds and paid retargeting that converts — without the two working against each other.

/ The approach
STEP 01

Intent keyword tracking

Owned targeted SEO intent-keyword research and tracking to focus effort on terms that actually convert.

STEP 02

On-page & content structuring

Optimized on-page and structured content across the portfolio to lift rankings and readability.

STEP 03

Link building

Built backlinks to strengthen authority and compound organic traffic over time.

STEP 04

Paid retargeting

Ran retargeting campaigns to recover funnel drop-off and turn organic interest into inbound inquiries.

/ Outcomes

Organic and paid, pulling together.
