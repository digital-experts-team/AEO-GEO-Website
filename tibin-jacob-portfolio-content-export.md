3. **Each case study** includes a "Next Case Study" link to the next one in sequence:
   - Growth Stacks → Heurist AI → Sonic DeFi → Aretto → Stock Market Kerala → Zoople & Webcastle → (back to all case studies)
4. **Case study pages** link back to the homepage via the nav logo ("← Tibin Jacob").
5. **Services page** links to relevant case studies as proof points (e.g., AEO service → Aretto case study, GEO service → Growth Stacks).
6. **Blog posts** cross-link to relevant case studies and the Services page.
7. **About page** links to all case studies and the Services page.
8. **All pages** include breadcrumb navigation (Home > Case Studies > [Case Study Name]).
9. **Contextual anchor text** — use keyword-rich anchor text (e.g., "See how I achieved 56% AI mention rate for Aretto" instead of "click here").

**02 — Owned Content (AEO)**
Built answer-first blog architecture targeting high commercial-intent queries. Restructured FAQs into extractable Q&A blocks and published comparison guides naming competitors so pages are classified as neutral editorial roundups by retrieval systems.

**03 — Earned Consensus (GEO)**
Leveraged Aretto's Shark Tank India S3 appearance and funding rounds (₹4.5Cr with celebrity investor Hardik Pandya) to trigger cross-domain news syndication (Inc42, YourStory). This established the independent consensus LLMs require.
