CASE STUDY Sonic DeFi · Full-time Mar 2022 — Sept 2023

Growing a DeFi product where its community lives.

Drove search visibility and ranking for Sonic's DeFi/AMM product on the Internet Computer — building and distributing AEO/GEO-optimized content across Reddit and X to strengthen organic citations and brand presence in both traditional and LLM-driven search.

ROLE
AI System Automation
SECTOR
DeFi / AMM · Web3
CHANNELS
Reddit · Twitter / X
RESULT
Stronger organic citations
/ The challenge

In Web3, credibility is earned in the community — not on a landing page.

A young DeFi/AMM product on the Internet Computer needed organic discoverability and trust. Its audience lived on Reddit and X, and increasingly asked LLMs for recommendations — so visibility had to be built where those conversations and citations actually formed.

/ The approach
STEP 01

Community-native content

Created AEO/GEO-optimized content tuned for r/dfinity and related communities — answering the questions the audience was already asking.

STEP 02

Distribution across Reddit & X

Placed content where it built citable, referenceable footprints — the sources LLMs and search both draw on.

STEP 03

Growth marketing paired in

Ran growth initiatives alongside the content push to expand product awareness and deepen community engagement.

STEP 04

Reinforced brand presence

Strengthened the brand's presence in both traditional search and LLM-driven answers through consistent, credible signals.
