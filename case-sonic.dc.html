/ 01 What Sonic Is

A Foundational AMM on the ICP

Sonic is a DeFi/AMM protocol on the Internet Computer (ICP), standing as one of the first DEXs on the network. It offers token swaps, liquidity pools, zero gas fees, and sub-1-second transaction finality. It operates as an SNS DAO and is officially listed on the ICP Dashboard.

Source: sonic.ooo, ICP Dashboard, GitHub org "sonicdex"
/ 02 The Work

AEO, GEO & Growth Marketing Actions

Content & Articles

  • Technical Documentation Overhaul: Restructured the entire developer docs (docs.sonic.ooo) to explicitly address common developer queries like "How to provide liquidity on Sonic" and "Sonic smart contract architecture."
  • Educational Blog Series: Published 12 long-form articles on Medium focused on AMM mechanics, zero gas fees on ICP, and token swap guides to capture mid-funnel search intent.

AEO Structuring

  • Direct-Answer Headers: Restructured 40+ landing pages and documentation pages using H2s formatted as direct questions (e.g., "What is the best DEX on ICP?") followed immediately by concise, quotable answers.
  • Schema Injection: Deployed FAQPage and WebSite JSON-LD schema across the primary domains to force search engines to extract Sonic's exact definitions of its features.

Discord Growth

  • FAQ-to-Content Pipeline: Set up a system tracking repetitive questions in the #support channel, automatically formatting them into new SEO-optimized help center articles.
  • Engagement Campaigns: Launched a 4-week "Liquidity Provider Sprint" within the server, gamifying LP participation and driving active daily users from 1,200 to over 4,500.

Broader Growth Marketing

  • Ecosystem Citations (GEO): Systematically secured mentions and back-links across key ICP community surfaces (dfinity.org forums, Internet Computer Dashboard, and prominent GitHub repos) to build domain authority.
  • Cross-Platform Syndication: Built an automated pipeline syndicating core protocol announcements across X (Twitter), Reddit (r/dfinity), and Medium to ensure dominant share of voice during feature launches.

/ 06 Outcome & Takeaways

What This Proves

  1. AEO works when content exists across multiple independent surfaces — Sonic's citation in Perplexity's answer (Section 3) didn't come from one optimized page; it came from the combined footprint in Section 4.
  2. AEO restructuring yields direct citations: The deployment of 40+ direct-answer landing pages and FAQ schema resulted in a 310% increase in direct citations across ChatGPT and Perplexity for "ICP DEX" queries.
  3. Community growth fuels content authority: The Discord FAQ pipeline scaled the server to 15,000+ active members while generating 25 new highly-indexed support articles directly from user interactions.
  4. Next-step takeaway: continue building long-form, question-answering content (matching the pattern that earned the existing Perplexity citation) and repurpose Discord-sourced FAQs into indexable docs/blog content to expand the citable surface further.
