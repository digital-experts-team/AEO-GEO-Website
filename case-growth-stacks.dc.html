/ The challenge

Being ranked isn't enough — brands need to be cited by the AI that answers.

Clients were losing ground as search shifted from blue links to AI-generated answers. Their content ranked, but generative engines rarely referenced them, mis-described their products, or credited competitors. The goal: engineer consistent, accurate citations across every major AI engine — and prove it with data.

/ Strategy — three pillars
01

Entity authority

JSON-LD schema — Organization, Product, FAQ — to strengthen the brand's footprint in Google's Knowledge Graph and make it machine-legible.

02

Topical clusters

Answer-first content clusters mapped to real search & prompt intent — structured so LLMs can lift, quote, and attribute cleanly.

03

Measured citations

A live dashboard tracking answer placements & citations across engines — turning visibility into something you can manage, not guess at.

/ The approach
STEP 01

Technical SEO audit

Crawl, index, and render analysis (Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, SEMrush) to fix the foundations that gate both search and AI readability.

STEP 02

Schema & entity build-out

Deployed JSON-LD across key templates and reconciled entities so engines resolve the brand consistently in the Knowledge Graph.

STEP 03

Answer-first content clusters

Built topical clusters targeting the questions users actually ask AI — structured for snippet and citation capture.

STEP 04

Track, iterate, report

Monitored citations, rankings, and content performance in one dashboard — feeding weekly iteration and clear client reporting.

/ Outcomes

Visibility you can measure.

30–40%
