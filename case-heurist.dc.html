CASE STUDY Heurist AI · Full-time Oct 2023 — Nov 2024

AI agents that watch how brands are cited.

Built internal AI agents that continuously prompt-tested ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini — monitoring how the brand and its competitors were cited, ranked, and described, and turning it into metrics leadership could act on.

/ The challenge

Nobody knew what the AI was actually saying about the brand.

LLM answers were becoming a primary discovery surface, but the brand's presence there was a black box — un-tracked, inconsistent, and impossible to report on. Leadership needed a reliable, repeatable read on citations, sentiment, and share-of-voice against competitors.

/ The approach
STEP 01

Prompt library design

Engineered a structured library of prompts mirroring real buyer questions, so tests were consistent and comparable over time.

STEP 02

Automated multi-LLM agents

Agents ran the library across ChatGPT, Perplexity & Gemini on a schedule, capturing raw responses for brand and competitors alike.

STEP 03

Structured metric extraction

Parsed raw outputs into citation counts, sentiment, and share-of-voice — normalized into a clean, trend-able schema.

STEP 04

Automated dashboards

Piped metrics into live dashboards giving leadership real-time visibility into AI-answer positioning.
